FAQ

How many people are in a class?
Minimum 3, maximum 8. Our small group classes allow for a multi-course meal to be cooked by each member and shared by all. Private lessons available.
Can I still join if I don't live near any international food markets?
Sure. You can join our virtual classes. "Shop" with us remotely, and watch us make the dish of your choice. We can flash-freeze each dinner course and ship it overnight.
